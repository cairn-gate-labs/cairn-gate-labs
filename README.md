# Cairn Gate Labs

**Independent AI systems lab.** We build practical tools, proprietary architectures, and advisory systems that help organizations turn AI capability into reliable, auditable workflows.

The bottleneck in AI adoption is not model capability. It is the gap between what models can do and what organizations can actually build, trust, and maintain — the workflow selection, data readiness, evaluation discipline, and governance that separates a demo from a working system.

---

## What we build

- **AI workflow diagnostics** — identify where AI creates measurable value before committing to a build
- **Memory architecture systems** — proprietary multi-scale memory for AI agents and workflows
- **Evaluation harnesses** — measure what "working" means before deployment, monitor it after
- **Technical advisory** — architecture reviews and implementation partnerships for founders and operators

## Who we work with

- Technical operators building AI-augmented workflows inside mid-market companies
- Founders and technical co-founders who need honest architecture reviews
- Organizations with compliance constraints who need AI systems that are auditable by design

## What we are not

Not an AI agency. Not a thought-leadership platform. Not a venture studio.
