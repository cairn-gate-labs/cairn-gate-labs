# Cairn Gate Labs

**Independent AI systems lab.**

We build practical tools, proprietary architectures, and advisory systems that help organizations turn AI capability into reliable, auditable workflows.

---

## The problem we work on

Most organizations now have access to capable AI models. Almost none have the infrastructure to use them reliably — the workflow selection, data readiness, evaluation discipline, and governance that separates a demo from a working system.

The bottleneck is not model capability. It is the gap between what models can do and what organizations can actually build, trust, and maintain.

---

## What we build

**Workflow diagnostics**
Map processes, surface automation opportunities, assess data readiness. Identify where AI creates measurable value before committing to a build.

**Memory architecture systems**
Proprietary multi-scale memory for AI agents and workflows — episodic, semantic, and procedural layers with provenance-preserving recall and auditable context reconstruction.

**Evaluation harnesses**
Define what "working" means before deployment. Measure it systematically. Monitor it after.

**Technical advisory**
Architecture reviews and implementation partnerships for founders and operators who need honest answers, not sales pitches.

---

## Who we work with

- **Technical operators** building AI-augmented workflows inside mid-market companies
- **Founders and technical co-founders** who need rigorous architecture reviews and capable implementation partners
- **Compliance-constrained organizations** who need AI systems that are auditable and provenance-preserving by design

---

## What we are not

We are not an AI agency — we do not white-label models or deliver generic chatbot builds.

We are not a thought-leadership platform — we publish when we have something to say that others can't.

We are not a venture studio — we incubate our own work, not other people's startups.
